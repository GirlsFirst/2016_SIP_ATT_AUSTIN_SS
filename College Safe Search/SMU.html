Policies

Southern Methodist University’s Title IX Harassment Policy prohibits all sexual misconduct, including “sexual harassment, gender-based harassment, sexual violence, sexual assault, dating violence, domestic violence, stalking and sexual exploitation.” According to University policy, “a sexual interaction is considered consensual when individuals willingly and knowingly engage in the interaction.” Consent is ongoing and can be withdrawn at any time. Consent to previous sexual acts does not imply consent to present or future sexual acts. The University encourages survivors to seek medical attention immediately after attack, regardless of if they want to pursue legal action against their attacker. They also assist survivors by providing confidential counseling, no contact orders, and accommodations for transportation and housing. Learn more.
