Policies

“Texas A&M University strives to maintain a work and educational environment free from discrimination, sexual harassment, and related retaliation in accordance with applicable federal and state laws. Individuals are encouraged to report all unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature.” The University encourages victims to seek immediate medical attention, consider long-term psychological treatment, and to explore taking legal and disciplinary action against their attacker.
